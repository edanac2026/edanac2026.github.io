ABOUT US

Girlbosses in STEM is a monthly publication that spotlights women in STEM and their contributions. We aim to share stories that inspire the youth, especially young girls, to pursue careers in the field of science. We cover the latest breakthroughs and their proponents, but we also look back on groundbreaking moments in history. We hope to facilitate interactions between the story writers and the readers.
