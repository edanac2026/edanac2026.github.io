ICON OF THE MONTH: HEDY LAMARR

Hedy Lamarr was a prominent actress in the 30s to late 50s Hollywood scene, but there was more to her than what met the eye. Lamarr had talent, beauty, and brains. She laid out the foundation for the technology of the modern-day Bluetooth, GPS, and Wi-Fi.
