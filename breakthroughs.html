FEMALE PROFESSOR WINS 'OUTSTANDING CONTRIBUTION TO STEM' AWARD

University professor and microbiologist Katie Laird, who looked into how the behavior of the COVID-19 virus on textiles, has just been awarded a regional science prize.

The De Montfort University professor and researcher bagged the Outstanding Contribution to STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics) award at the 25th Enterprising Women Awards, which had over 100 candidates.

The awards show is hosted by East Midlands Chamber, with the goal of recognizing the contributions of women as business leaders and entrepreneurs.
